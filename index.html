Good Ally Reading Group

An informal gathering for discussing and learning from writing by ibpoc/qt writers.

how it works

Each meeting is capped at 25 people to keep things intimate. You can RSVP on Eventbrite a week before the event.

A reader will pick 1-3 articles that can be read in one sitting. We'll sit in a circle and everyone will takes turns reading. Then we'll discuss.

The structure of this group was inspired by a reading group led by Martine Syms

details

BYO food! BYO Indoor shoes! I'll be providing some snacks but since it's dinner time, it's probably a good idea to bring something for yourself to eat. There is coffee/tap water at the space, and a gender neutral toilet. Unfortunately it isn’t accessible and there’s 5 steps to take down to the semi-underground diy venue.

The event is free; the Brandscape always appreciates donations.

get updates

Stay updated on upcoming meetings! I try to rotate the days it's organized on so different folks can attend.
